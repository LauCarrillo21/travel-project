Why Mérida?

Mérida, "the White City"

Mérida is distinguished for being a city full of peace, calm and for its kind and hospitable people.

It is the ideal place for those who want to spend a vacation in a relaxed atmosphere and with a warm climate. Mérida was founded on January 6, 1542, and built on the Mayan city of Toh , which can be seen in the surroundings of its Plaza Grande, since its history, influence and culture were reflected in its architecture. Walking through its streets in an atmosphere of history and fun is what our beautiful Mérida offers you.

Awesome places to visit

Archaeological Sites

The Yucatan Peninsula is home to about 100 public Mayan Ruins in Mexico.

Chichen Itzá

If you want to visit Chichen Itza from Merida it will take around 1.5 hours.

A Wonder of the World

Chichén Itzá is a well-known archaeological site, even more after being selected as one of the New 7 Wonders of the World on July 7, 2007.

Chichén Itzá is beautiful and magnificent as you may have already heard and it has many Mayan ruins to visit and learn about its history.

Amazing Cenotes

In the Yucatan Peninsula there are at least 10,000 cenotes, which are a pool of water or a natural pond that only exist in few places in the world.

Cenote X-batún

It is located 49 km. from the city of Merida

The most interesting natural phenomenon of Yucatán

It is an open type cenote, which is approximately 20 meters deep. It is suitable for swimming and cave diving.

This cenote has a special scenic view due to the tree roots that hang over the entrance of the cave.
